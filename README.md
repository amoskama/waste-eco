♻️ Waste IQ EcoTrack
Real-time AI Purity Verification for a Circular Economy
Waste IQ EcoTrack is an AI-powered ecosystem designed to bridge the gap between households, waste aggregators, and recyclers. By using high-resolution computer vision and edge computing, we intercept contamination at the source—the bin—ensuring that recyclable materials maintain their maximum market value and stay out of landfills.

🌟 The Vision
The climate crisis is driven by broken waste systems. Contamination in recycling streams leads to millions of tons of waste being diverted to landfills, polluting our land and water. Waste IQ EcoTrack provides the "real-time data link" needed to build trust across the supply chain and accelerate the transition to a circular economy.

🛠️ Technical Stack
Backend: Python 3.13, Flask API.

AI/ML: TensorFlow, Keras, MobileNetV2 (Transfer Learning).

Frontend: Modern HTML5, CSS3 (Custom Uber-style Design System), Vanilla JavaScript.

Environment: Linux
